## Username Convention

### Students:
Format: `{first_letter_of_firstname}{lastname}{optional_number}`
Example: 
- Piotr Adamek → `padamek` or `p.adamek`
- Jan Chlondowski → `jchlondowski` or `j.chlondowski`

### Teachers:
Format: `{acronym_lowercase}` or `{firstname}.{lastname}`
Example:
- Edyta Kozicka (EK) → `ek` or `e.kozicka`
- Piotr Muszyński (Mu) → `mu` or `p.muszynski`
